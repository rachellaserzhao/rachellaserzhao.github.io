Simulation of protein in water

My undergraduate thesis used a computational method to model the process of aggregation of Tau proteins which is one of the hallmarks of Alzheimer's disease. A computational modeling approach was used to reveal structural, energetic, and dynamic information of tau protein at the molecular level.
