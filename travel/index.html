Roaming with Purpose

Grazing so calmly and slow,
On a Norwegian highway,
with a mountain view,
A peaceful scene, the perfect hue.
                  
Tent pitched on mountain top
Nature's peace surrounds me here
Starry sky above.
                  
Steam rises from bowl
Warm and comforting aroma
Mushroom soup delights.
                  
Concrete jungle thrives
People rush, buildings soar high
Nature's voice, unheard.
                  
Quiet ritual starts,
Green tea in hand, hearts at peace,
Serenity flows.
                  
In mountain's embrace,
Onsen's warm waters soothe,
Tension melts away.
                  
City of light,
The Eiffel Tower stands,
A beacon shining bright,
Inviting all to visit.
                  
A symbol of a time,
When tension filled the air,
A reminder of a past fear.
                  
Oktoberfest roars,
Bavarian traditions live,
Beer and laughter fill the air. 
                  
The ocean's breeze,
A symphony of salt air,
A taste of summer, grand.
